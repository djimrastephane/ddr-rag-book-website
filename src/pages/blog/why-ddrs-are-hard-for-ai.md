Daily Drilling Reports look structured, but they're not. The same event gets written three
different ways by three different crews: *stuck pipe*, *tight hole*, *high torque*. A keyword
search finds one of them and silently misses the rest.

## The three core problems

- **Format chaos** — digital PDFs, scanned pages, and mixed layouts in a single archive.
- **Oilfield shorthand** — `POOH`, `BHA`, `WOB`, `NPT`: dense abbreviations no general-purpose model reads reliably.
- **Terminology drift** — the vocabulary for the same operation changes across contractors, crews, and years.

The book's early chapters tackle each of these directly, with real Utah FORGE reports.
